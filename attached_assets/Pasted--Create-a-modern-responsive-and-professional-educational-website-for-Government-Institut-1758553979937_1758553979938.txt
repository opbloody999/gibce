

**Create a modern, responsive, and professional educational website** for
**Government Institute of Business & Commercial Education (GIBCE), Khairpur Mir’s, Sindh, Pakistan.**

### Design Guidelines

* **Color palette:** Primary: navy blue `#1E3A8A`; Accent: gold `#D4AF37`; Neutral: white `#FFFFFF`.
* **Fonts:** Google Fonts → Poppins (headings), Open Sans (body). Use consistent font sizing.
* **Layout:** Clean, minimal, section-based single-page site. Use plenty of white space.
* **Animations:** Subtle only (fade-in, smooth scroll). Avoid rainbow, flashing, or bounce effects.
* **Responsiveness:** Must look good on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
* **Navigation:** Sticky top navbar with links to Home, About, Courses, Admission, Downloads, Contact.

### Content Sections

1. **Hero Section**

   * Background image (placeholder campus/students).
   * Welcome text + institution name.
   * Call-to-action button (“Apply Now”).

2. **About Section**

   * Short history and mission.
   * Highlights / achievements.

3. **Courses Section**

   * Grid layout with 3–4 cards (DIT, CIT, OAT).
   * Each card: course name, duration, short description.

4. **Faculty/Staff Section**

   * Cards with photo, name, designation, short bio.

5. **Gallery Section**

   * Responsive grid gallery (placeholder images).
   * Simple hover zoom (no rainbow borders).

6. **Downloads Section**

   * Cards/list for admission form, syllabus, templates, etc.

7. **Testimonials / Achievements**

   * Simple carousel or 3 cards with student photo + text.

8. **Contact Section**

   * Contact form (name, email, message).
   * Google Maps embed.
   * Social media links.

9. **Footer**

   * Quick links, contact info, copyright.

### Technical Notes

* Use **plain HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript** (no frameworks unless explicitly needed).
* Keep **CSS clean and professional** — flat design, no rainbow/gradient overload.
* Include **preloader animation** (simple spinner, single color).
* Add **back-to-top button** (small, bottom-right, minimal style).
* Ensure **accessibility** (alt text, proper contrast, keyboard navigation).
* Use **placeholder text/images** where content isn’t available.
